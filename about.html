MANIL JHAVERI

Hi, I'm Manil, an advertising enthusiast and creative thinker based in London. Originally from India, with stints in Leicester, I've been immersed in diverse cultural contexts that have shaped my approach to advertising.

Having studied media and advertising while working various design and advertising roles, I've honed my skills in creating innovative and audience-centric campaigns. My passion for visual storytelling, combined with my background in photography, allows me to craft compelling narratives that resonate with audiences on a deeper level.

Outside of the advertising world, I'm also a skilled dhol instrument player, bringing rhythm and energy to any project or collaboration. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for capturing moments, I strive to create impactful campaigns that leave a lasting impression.

I believe advertising goes beyond selling products; it's about solving problems creatively and fostering societal change. My goal is to move away from data-centric approaches and focus on crafting impactful, long-term solutions that resonate with consumers.
